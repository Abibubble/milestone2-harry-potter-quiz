How To Play

  • Choose your House on the homepage to play the quiz.
  • Once the quiz starts, a timer for 120 seconds begins.
  • You'll be shown a question, and four possible answers.
  • Click the answer you believe is correct.
  • If it's correct, your score beneath will update.
  • If it's incorrect, your score beneath will not update.
  • If you'd like to exit the quiz, you can click on any of the buttons at the top of the screen.

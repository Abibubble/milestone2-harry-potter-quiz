How To Play

  • Choose your House on the homepage to play the quiz.
  • Once the quiz starts, you'll be shown a question, and four possible answers.
  • Click the answer you believe is correct.
  • If it's correct, your score beneath will update, and a sound effect will play.
  • If it's incorrect, your score beneath will not update, and a sound effect will play
  • If you'd like to exit the quiz, you can click on the 'Home' button at the top of the screen.
